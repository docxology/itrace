# iTrace v0.4.1

iTrace v0.4.1 is the diagnostic v1 release candidate for the MIT-licensed
webcam eye-movement analysis toolkit at <https://github.com/docxology/itrace>.

## Scope

- Pure NumPy/SciPy core for gaze geometry, saccade/fixation/microsaccade
  detection, pupil preprocessing, pupil phase, scanpath statistics,
  calibration, quality diagnostics, and benchmark scoring.
- Optional webcam/MediaPipe capture shell, local HTML/WebSocket orchestrator,
  Streamlit dashboard, CLI workflows, generated figures, and rendered
  manuscript.
- Five-session, single-participant, single-device diagnostic pilot with two
  conditions and zero reference-backed rows.

## Evidence Boundary

This release does not claim reference-device accuracy, cross-device validity,
population generality, or webcam-accuracy validation. The empirical evidence is
diagnostic and local; reference-device, public-dataset, or derived manual
annotation evidence remains future validation scope.
